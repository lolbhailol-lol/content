## The Opportunity

### Problem

India's youth event ecosystem is massive but invisible:

- **40M+ college students** across thousands of campuses
- **Thousands of events** every week: fests, competitions, workshops
- **Exploding outdoor community**: treks, run clubs, marathons
- **Growing arts scene**: theatre, open mics, poetry

Yet there's **no platform to discover any of it**. Events are scattered across:
- WhatsApp groups (invite-only, unsearchable)
- Instagram stories (algorithm-gated, ephemeral)
- College notice boards (offline, local)
- Word of mouth (slow, limited)

Students miss events they would have loved. Organizers can't reach audiences beyond their network. Communities grow slowly because discovery is broken.

### Solution

CrwdCtrl is the **single app** where:
1. **Students** discover events, fests, communities near them
2. **Organizers** list, manage, and get paid for events
3. **Communities** grow with tools for recurring engagement

**Discovery → Registration → Payment → Check-in** — all in one platform.

### Why Now?

1. **Post-COVID return to IRL**: Young people crave real-world experiences
2. **UPI/digital payments mainstream**: Frictionless transactions possible
3. **Smartphone penetration**: Every student has a phone
4. **No incumbent**: The market is waiting to be consolidated

## Business Model

### Primary Revenue: Platform Fee

- **3% platform fee** on paid registrations
- User pays ticket price + fee
- Organizer receives ticket price (minus payment processor)
- CrwdCtrl retains fee

**Example**: ₹500 ticket → User pays ₹515 → CrwdCtrl earns ₹15

### Unit Economics (Projected)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Avg. ticket price | ₹300–500 |
| Platform fee per transaction | ₹9–15 |
| Gross margin | ~85% (after GST) |

## The Narrative

### The Hook

> "40 million college students. Thousands of events every week. No way to find any of them. CrwdCtrl is the discovery layer."

### The Story

A student at MIT-WPU kept missing events — fests, treks, meetups — not because he didn't want to go, but because he never knew they were happening. WhatsApp groups are scattered. Instagram is algorithmic noise. Posters get ignored.

So he built CrwdCtrl: the one app where you can see everything happening near you. Register, pay, show up. Simple.

Now he's proving the model at his own campus, onboarding organizers, processing registrations, and building the flywheel that will scale city by city, campus by campus.

### The Vision

CrwdCtrl becomes **the default layer** between young India and real-world experiences:

- Every campus fest runs on CrwdCtrl
- Every run club is discovered on CrwdCtrl
- Every trek group recruits on CrwdCtrl
- Every student opens CrwdCtrl to see "what's happening this weekend"
